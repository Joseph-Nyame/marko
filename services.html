Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

How do you test the purity of gold?

We use advanced X-ray fluorescence (XRF) technology and acid scratch tests, conducted by certified professionals, to accurately determine the purity of your gold, ensuring fair and transparent evaluations.

How can I verify the authenticity of gold purchased from Bethel Gold?

All gold products come with a certificate of authenticity. We also provide detailed documentation of purity and weight, verified through our rigorous testing methods, and offer consultations to explain our processes.

What payment methods do you accept?

For purchases, we accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and secure online payments. For gold sales, we offer instant payments via bank transfer, cash, or check, ensuring quick and secure transactions.
